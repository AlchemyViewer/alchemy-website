## Question

How do I hide everyone except my friends?

## Answer

To show only your friends and hide all other avatars, you need to enable this specific setting:
Top Menu → Develop → Avatar → Character Tests → Render Only Friends

![Screenshot of the menu showing where the setting toggle is for "Render Only friends"](./images/hide-everyone-except-friends/Screenshot_Render_Only_Friends.avif)

## Details

You will need to enable the Develop menu for this, which runs the risk of accidentally triggering certain hotkeys. It's important to disable the Develop menu when you do not need it.

This setting will reset itself after logging out.

To enable the Develop menu:

1. Enable the Advanced menu with menu World → Show → Advanced Menu
2. Under the Advanced menu, click "Show Develop menu" or use the hotkey Ctrl+Alt+Q.
